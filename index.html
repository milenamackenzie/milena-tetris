How to Play

Use controls to move and rotate pieces:

  • R - Rotate piece clockwise
  • A / ← - Move piece left
  • S / ↓ - Move piece down faster
  • D / → - Move piece right
  • Space - Pause/Resume game

Clear lines to score points! Speed increases every 5 lines.
